# 📊 Amazon Prime Video Data Analysis Report

## 📌 Objective

To analyze Amazon Prime Video content data and extract insights related to content quality, popularity, genres, and talent impact.

---

## 📂 Dataset Overview

* Titles dataset (~9000 entries)
* Credits dataset (~124k entries)
* Includes information about ratings, genres, cast, and production

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

### 1. Content Distribution

* Movies dominate over TV shows
* Drama and Comedy are the most common genres

### 2. Content Growth

* Rapid increase in content over recent years
* No significant improvement in average ratings

### 3. Ratings vs Popularity

* Weak positive correlation between IMDb votes and ratings
* Popular content is not always high quality

### 4. Talent Impact

* Actors and directors influence quality
* However, frequency does not guarantee better ratings

### 5. Content Segmentation

* Content classified into:

  * Premium
  * Hidden Gems
  * Overhyped
  * Low Impact

---

## 💡 Business Insights

* Focus on high-performing genres
* Invest in quality over quantity
* Identify and promote hidden gems
* Balance popularity with storytelling quality

---

## 🧾 Conclusion

Amazon Prime’s content library is diverse and rapidly growing, but maintaining consistent quality remains a challenge. Data-driven strategies can help improve content selection, audience engagement, and overall platform performance.
